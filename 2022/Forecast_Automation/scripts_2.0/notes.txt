Group Quarter vs Non-Group Quarter Information: 

Raw MGRA Files: 
- HH (Total number of households): does not include GQ 
- hhp: total household population (exclude gq pop) 

Households file: 
- unittype
    - 0 = Non-GQ Households
    - 1 = GQ Household
